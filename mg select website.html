  1. OUR HERITAGE

A heritage written in motion.

2025
+ MG Select – A New Era of Luxury
Leading the future of automotive luxury, JSW MG Motor India unveils MG Select, its exclusive luxury channel brand that sets the stage for curated experiences.
2025
+ Racing In - The MG Cyberster
Introducing the world’s fastest MG - the MG Cyberster. Crafted for the thrill-seeker, it redefines the boundaries of speed and innovation.
2025
+ Unveiling the MG M9
The MG M9 – a new standard in luxury, unveiled as the Presidential Limousine. A perfect fusion of luxury and comfort, created for the discerning.
1924
+ A STAR IS BORN​
The first MG arrives in the world – the Old Number One.
1930
+ THE BIRTH OF MG CAR CLUB
Community. Camaraderie. Cars. The MG Car Club roared to life, starting with 30 enthusiastic members at the Roebuck Hotel.
1933
+ IT'S A WINNER ​
The MG K3 Magnette emerges triumphant at the Mille Miglia, one of the toughest races in the world at the time.
1945
+ A BRITISH ICON, AN AMERICAN SENSATION
The MG TC, complete with latest features and gadgets, starts the American craze for the British sports car.​
1956
+ AN ALL-TIME CLASSIC​
The MG ZB Magnette quickly became a beloved classic, captivating the hearts of many.
1957
+ WORLD RECORD CONQUERED ​
The MG EX181, a blur of motion, pushes the boundaries of speed, reaching an astonishing 109.81 m/s and claiming a new landspeed record at Bonneville Salt Flats by Sir Stirling Moss!
1959
+ MGA: MORE POWER, MORE GLORY
Building on the MGA 1500's success, MG unleashed the MGA, a 1588cc powerhouse that sent drivers soaring.
1962
+ ROYAL CONNECTIONS​
The MGB Roadster, a modern classic with monocoque construction, reigned supreme as MG's best-seller. Even royalty was smitten, with Prince Charles choosing it’s bigger brother, the MGC.
1973
+ SMALL CAR, BIG POWER
MG unleashed the power of the Rover V8 engine with the MG BGT V8, following the lead of independent tuners like Ken Costello.
1985
+ BORN TO RALLY
The successful Metro paved the way for the 6R4, a 410bhp beast born from the spirit of the rallying era.
1995
+ THE REBIRTH OF COOL
Mid-engine, gas suspension, global sensation: The MGF arrived and conquered.
2000
+ 21ST CENTURY IN MOTION​
MG ushers in the new millennium with the MG TF, followed by the MG ZR, and two sports saloons; The MG ZS and MG ZT. 
2012
+ A WINNING FORMULA
The MG 6 GT, with Plato at the helm, dominated, securing 5 BTCC championships and 6 race wins.
2018
+ THE INDIA CHAPTER
MG paved its way to India, making a resounding entry, ready to make its mark.
2018
+ A Bold Beginning
MG Motor India’s journey takes off with the launch of its state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Halol, Gujarat.
